Alabama Mining Encyclopedia is a standalone historical mining intelligence repository for Walker County, Alabama.

The project preserves and connects records about coal mines, mine companies, coal camps, company towns, rail infrastructure, historic mine maps, mine disasters, industrial sites, and related communities.

It is separate from the Walker County Almanac and Walker County Atlas. The Almanac preserves article-based history. The Atlas maps places. Alabama Mining Encyclopedia owns the mining-specific data, registry structure, and future interactive mine map.
