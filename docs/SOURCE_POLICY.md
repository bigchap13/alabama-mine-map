# Source Policy

Do not treat a mining claim as verified unless it is supported by a source.

Preferred source levels:

1. Official mine map repositories
2. State or federal mining/geology records
3. Historical maps
4. County histories
5. Newspaper archives
6. Cemetery, church, school, or company records
7. Oral history or local memory, clearly marked as unverified

Unverified claims must remain in research notes or research_needed fields until supported.
