# Walker County Mining

Walker County Mining is the dedicated mining-history and mine-intelligence system for Walker County, Alabama.

It documents coal mines, mining companies, coal camps, railroad connections, mine maps, mining communities, industrial sites, mine disasters, production history, and related historical geography.

## Ecosystem Role

- Walker County Atlas answers: Where is it?
- Walker County Almanac answers: What is it?
- Walker County Mining answers: What was mined there, who mined it, and what did it connect to?
- Walker County Cold Case Encyclopedia answers: What happened?
- Walker County Hub connects the systems.

## Initial Scope

- Mine registry
- Mining company registry
- Coal camp registry
- Historic mine maps
- Railroad and spur connections
- Mine disasters and fatal incidents
- Coal seams and geology references
- Company housing and industrial sites
- Cross-links to Atlas places and Almanac communities
